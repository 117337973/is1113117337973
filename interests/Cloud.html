Cloud Computing

Introduction

The term "cloud computing" is everywhere. It's highly possible that you are using cloud services in some way right now. Services like e-mail, streaming music or even online gaming makes use of cloud technology behind the scenes. The term 'Cloud' is just a metaphor for the internet.

In short, cloud computing is storing and accessing data and programs over the Internet instead of your computer's hard drive.
