Cloud Computing

Introduction

The term "cloud computing" is everywhere. It's highly possible that you are using cloud services in some way right now. Services like e-mail, streaming music or even online gaming makes use of cloud technology behind the scenes. The term 'Cloud' is just a metaphor for the internet.

In short, cloud computing is storing and accessing data and programs over the Internet instead of your computer's hard drive.

Cloud Computing and Media

Cloud computing the driving foroce in massive changes in the media industry. YouTube, Netflix and spotify are just a few of the companies that are taking advantage of cloud computing capabilities.

Cloud computing enables media firms to reinvent their business models, fueling massive improvements in the delivery of high quality digital content, and providing ample resources to collect, store and process big data to help segment and target the market better.
